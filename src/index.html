What is this?

I've been thinking about sonification as a way to give an overview of a workspace, similar to what you get when you first open a project and either see a page full of blocks or an almost-empty workspace.

This page generates and then plays audio to represent a workspace. You can play with the tempo slider and use the scenario dropdown to load various sets of blocks.

Some ideas I want to explore next: different instruments; adjust tempo by number of blocks on the workspace; adjust tempo by number of blocks in a stack; add a "bridge" sound for statement inputs in the middle of a block.

Contact info: https://github.com/rachel-fenichel/music-workspace
